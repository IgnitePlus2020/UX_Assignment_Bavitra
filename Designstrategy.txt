Design Strategies

1.Make the brand noticeable with a logo
2.Navigation through the website should be easy
3.The user should be able to easily understand the website.
4.Do not complicate the design.
5.The homepage should be very informative.
6.Should be customizable for the user.
7.Should have constant promotion. brand deals,discounts etc
8.The user should be able to report any problem and provided with appropriate solutions.
